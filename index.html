What is SDG 3?

SDG 3 focuses on ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for everyone, at every age. It covers things like reducing maternal and child mortality, fighting infectious diseases, supporting mental health, and improving access to healthcare and medicines.

SDG 3 ‚Äî Good Health and Well-being

SDG 3 seeks to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all ages. Key aims include:

  • Reducing maternal mortality and ending preventable deaths of newborns and children.
  • Combating epidemics such as AIDS, tuberculosis, malaria, and neglected tropical diseases.
  • Reducing deaths from non-communicable diseases and promoting mental health.
  • Ensuring access to affordable essential medicines and vaccines.
  • Strengthening capacity for early warning, risk reduction and management of health risks.

These goals aim to create healthier societies where people can thrive and contribute to sustainable development.
